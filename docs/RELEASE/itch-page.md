**Title:** MOGUL — Hollywood 1933–1949

## Short pitch
Run a Hollywood studio through the Golden Age — where history is the
antagonist. Exploit the lawless pre-Code window before the censors
arrive. Lose your leading men to the draft. Decide what you'll say to
HUAC — and live with it. Survive the strikes, the television set, and
the Supreme Court, and let 1950 write your studio's epilogue.

## Bullets
- A tuned, dangerous economy: every campaign is winnable and losable;
  a simulation suite enforces it
- 151 historical events with real mechanical consequences, on the real
  calendar — attendance booms, strikes, the Paramount Decree
- The Hays Code as gameplay: a genuine pre-Code window, then sixteen
  years of negotiation
- HUAC: name names or refuse — both choices follow you to the epilogue
- Era-scored generative soundtrack: hot jazz to war brass to noir
- Every campaign different: shifting script markets, talent debuts,
  7 scenario starts, conduct-aware endings
- 100% offline; saves in your browser (desktop build: on disk)

## Disclosure box (required)
All studio personnel and film titles are fictional creations evoking
the era's archetypes. Historical events and public figures appear in
news context as historical record.
